# Baoshan City Commercial-to-Provident Fund Loan Conversion (商转公) Policy

**Source:** 保山市人民政府门户网站 (baoshan.gov.cn)
**Document:** 《保山市商业性个人住房贷款转住房公积金个人住房贷款管理办法（试行）》
**Published:** 2023-09-08
**Effective:** 2023-10-07 to 2026-10-06
**Search query used on baoshan.gov.cn:** `商业贷款转公积金贷款`

## Eligibility Checklist

1. Normal provident fund contributor (in or outside Baoshan) — flexible employment OK if contributing in Baoshan
2. Pure commercial housing loan (NOT a combined provident-fund+commercial loan)
3. Loan originated for housing purchased in Baoshan
4. Loan is current, with 12+ months of normal repayment history
5. No more than 3 cumulative overdue payments (逾期)
6. Borrower is the primary borrower or spouse on the commercial loan
7. Property title certificate (不动产权证书) must be obtained
8. Property is sole collateral for the commercial loan — no other liens, seizures, or residency rights
9. Title certificate lists only: borrower, spouse, adult children, or parents (no other parties)

## Housing Count Rules (住房套数)
- **1 home (any size):** eligible
- **2 homes:** only the one ≤144 sqm qualifies
- **3+ homes:** NOT eligible

## Loan Amount Limits
- **Dual-income (both spouses contributing):** max 600,000 RMB
- **Single-income:** max 400,000 RMB
- Must not exceed: remaining principal - 1 month's payment, rounded to nearest 1,000
- Constraint: commercial loan balance + any provident fund withdrawals for this property ≤ total purchase price

## Two Conversion Methods

### 1. Direct Transfer (贷款直转) — RECOMMENDED
- No self-financing needed to pay off commercial loan
- Provident fund center pays directly to original bank's designated account
- Can be cross-bank (original commercial loan bank ≠ provident fund bank)
- Requires: subordinated mortgage registration (顺位抵押) or mortgage change registration

### 2. Pay-First-Then-Convert (先还后转)
- Must self-finance to clear commercial loan first
- Then apply for provident fund loan
- Provident fund disburses to borrower's personal account after new mortgage registered

## Participating Banks (23 branches as of 2023)
ICBC (工行), CCB (建行), ABC (农行), BOC (中行), Longyang Rural Commercial Bank (隆阳农商行), Tengchong Rural Commercial Bank (腾冲农商行), Changning Rural Commercial Bank (昌宁农商行), Longling Rural Commercial Bank (龙陵农商行), Shidian Rural Commercial Bank (施甸农商行), Fudian Bank (富滇银行), Bank of Communications (交通银行) — with branches in various counties

## Dynamic Management Mechanism (CRITICAL)
- **Starts:** when provident fund loan rate < 75% AND trending downward
- **Stops:** when provident fund loan rate ≥ 85%
- This means the program may be suspended at any time — always verify current status

## Required Documents
1. ID cards, marriage certificate, household registration booklet (户口簿)
2. Class I savings account at the receiving bank
3. Property title certificate (copy)
4. Commercial loan contract, mortgage contract, loan voucher, last 12 months repayment history
5. **Commercial loan bank's consent letter for conversion** (商转公贷款同意书) — critical
6. Subordinated mortgage consent letter (顺位抵押登记同意书)
7. For异地 (out-of-city) contributors: provident fund info form
8. For military spouses: military ID + provident fund contribution proof
9. For parents/adult children as co-applicant: proof of relationship

## Loan Term
- Max 30 years total from original loan origination
- Cannot exceed borrower's statutory retirement age + 5 years
- Cannot exceed remaining term of original commercial loan

## Important Notes from Policy Interpretation
- Supports "又取又贷" pattern: borrower can have previously withdrawn provident fund for this property and still apply for conversion, as long as the balance constraint (loan + withdrawals ≤ purchase price) is met
- Direct transfer method does NOT require the same bank — cross-bank conversion is explicitly supported
- The policy interpretation document (政策解读) contains worked numerical examples that clarify edge cases
